Our co-investment strategy has resonated well with founders and the demonstration of our value add has enabled us to win highly competitive deals.

Our investment strategy is focused on companies with strong fundamentals and capital-efficiency. This has already started to yield good results

3 companies out of the 7 (Spotdraft, The Ayurveda Experience and Homeville) have raised follow-on capital resulting in significant markups in just 12 months.

While up rounds are a good early indicator of breakout companies, the uprounds in all 3 companies was followed with a 3x top-line growth.

As ecosystem initiatives, we hosted mixers for founders and investors across Delhi and Bangalore and took part in industry events such as India Global Forum.

Sponsored the India Global Forum event in Dubai that saw participation from 400+ guests from the start-up ecosystem. Satveer along with portfolio founder, Shashank from Spotdraft were copanellists and had an engaging discussion on “Borderless Ambitions — First-Hand Lessons on Building a Global Company”.

We also launched Riverwalk’s credits program for our portfolio companies. Through this program, we’re building a channel partnership stack to offer state-of-the-art tools to our portfolio companies. We have onboarded 20 such platforms with a combined free credits of ~$2.5M being issued to each of our portfolio companies.

We also launched our HR tech thesis report & a three-piece article on B2B Marketplace which was widely covered and has started appearing as reference points in startup fundraising decks.

All in all amidst the tricky macroeconomic conditions we’ve been focused on being disciplined and watchful. We’re strong believers that this is India’s decade and that the largest incremental gains will accrue to technology startups as India propels to a USD 10 trillion economy.

We’re committed to investing in innovative companies and look forward to working closely with you.
