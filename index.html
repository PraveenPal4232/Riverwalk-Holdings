While up rounds and markups are a good early signal of breakouts - this was followed with 3x top-line growth for all companies.

As ecosystem initiatives, we hosted industry events such as India Global Forum and mixers for founders and investors across Delhi and Bangalore.

Hosted a mixer in partnership with K-Capital to engage with the Delhi community, the event witnessed the participation of 70+ VCs.

Sponsored the India Global Forum event in Dubai that saw participation from 400+ guests from the start-up ecosystem. Satveer along with portfolio founder, Shashank from Spotdraft were co-panellists and had an engaging discussion on “Borderless Ambitions — First-Hand Lessons on Building a Global Company”.

We also launched Riverwalk’s Credits program for our portfolio companies. Through this program, we envision building a value-add channel partnership stack to offer state-of-the-art tools to our portfolio companies. We have onboarded 20 such platforms with a combined free credit of ~$2.5M being issued to each of the portfolio companies.

We have also launched our HR tech & 2B Marketplace report which was widely covered and has started appearing as reference points in startup fundraising decks.

All in all amidst the tricky macroeconomic conditions we’ve been focused on being disciplined and watchful.

We’re strong believers that this is India’s decade and that the largest incremental gains will accrue to technology startups as India accelerates to a USD 10 trillion GDP economy. We’re committed to investing in innovative and disruptive startups and look forward to the possibility of working with you.
